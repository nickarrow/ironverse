The Snakepit is a large settlement built within an asteroid.  It is home to the pirate clan known as [[The Void Cobras]].  Despite the fearsome reputation pirates have, The Snakepit is also home to a large number of non-raider crew - engineers, medics, etc. as well as the families of the raiders.
